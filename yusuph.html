About Me

My name is Joseph Kithaika, Kenyan. I come from Taita Taveta and I am a passionate web developer with a focus on creating accessible and user-friendly websites. I believe in the power of the web to connect people and share ideas.

With a background in education, design. I am committed to continuous learning and improving my craft.

Im familiar with html.

My goal is to build websites that not only look great but also provide a seamless experience for all users.

During my free time I explore on different technologies.

I also participate community work like visiting children's home, taking care of the environment, empowering boys and girls through Menstrual Hygiene Management and Mental Health talks.

Location

I am currently based in Thika Kiambu County, Kenya. I love the natural beauty of this region and draw inspiration from its landscapes.
